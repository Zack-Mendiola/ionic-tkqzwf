About the App

This app is an ideal mass calculator. It shows you the different ideal weights according to Robinson, Miller, Hamwi, and Devine.

How to Use the App

The app is easy to use and straightforward. Just input your height in inches and in centimeters then press calculate.

The Developers

Jaywynn Angelo C. Manlapaz John Zachary R. Mendiola Jerome Kenjho Jarabejo Pumaren

The developers of this app are from the company This Time Fitness. The purpose of this app is to inform people of the ideal weight they should have based on their height. Once they know their ideal weight, the company plans on helping these people achieve it.
